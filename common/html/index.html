Harmonization and Linked Data

We take records from different sources and make them available using RDF linked data, DCAT and SPARQL.

We use state-of-the-art technology to process different metadata records and make all the data available under a common scheme.

For more details or to cite this work please see:

  • Reconciling Heterogeneous Descriptions of Language Resources. John P. McCrae, Philipp Cimiano, Victor Rodriguez-Doncel, Daniel Vila-Suero, Jorge Gracia, Luca Matteis, Roberto Navigli, Andrejs Abele, Gabriela Vulcu and Paul Buitelaar
  • Linghub: a Linked Data based portal supporting the discovery of language resources. John P. McCrae and Philipp Cimiano
